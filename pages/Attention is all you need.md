		- Transduction problems refer to a class of tasks in machine learning and artificial intelligence where the goal is to map sequences to sequences, rather than performing simple classification or regression. These problems are particularly prevalent in natural language processing (NLP) and other sequential data tasks. Here are some common examples of transduction problems:
		- **Machine Translation**:
			- **Task**: Translating a sentence from one language to another.
			- **Example**: Translating "Hello, how are you?" from English to French as "Bonjour, comment ça va ?".
		- **Text Summarization**:
			- **Task**: Generating a concise summary of a longer text.
			- **Example**: Summarizing a news article into a few key sentences.
